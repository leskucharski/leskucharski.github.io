Coming late 2025/early 2026, hopefully.

This was meant to be an expedition into moderately-high fidelity Rock Music, as a vehicle for an autobiographical recount of the year I spent pissing into the wind after graduating from high school.

I had far too much happen to me, and did far too many things to myself, for any hope of any sort of simple one-to-one literal songwriting to feel at all practical/appropriate. The third album may end me.

0.25x0.25 is to be released as a Single beforehand.
